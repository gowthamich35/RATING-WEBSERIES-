DELHI CRIME

2019 ‧ Drama ‧ 2 seasons

Delhi Crime is an Indian police procedural crime drama streaming television series written and directed by Richie Mehta.[1][2] Creative manager Chirag Shah and produced by Golden Karaven, Ivanhoe Productions, Film Karavan and Poor Man's Productions. The series star Shefali Shah, Rasika Dugal, Adil Hussain and Rajesh Tailang.
