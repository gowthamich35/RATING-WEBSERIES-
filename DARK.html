DARK

2017 ‧ Mystery ‧ 3 seasons

Dark is a German science fiction thriller television series co-created by Baran bo Odar and Jantje Friese.[5][6][7] It ran for three seasons from 2017 to 2020. The story follows characters from the fictional town of Winden, Germany, as they pursue the truth in the aftermath of a child's disappearance. They follow connections between four estranged families to unravel a sinister time travel conspiracy that spans several generations.
